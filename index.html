Wash Your Hands!

Stay Healthy!

Remember to wash your hands regularly to stay clean and germ-free.

  • 1. Wet your hands with clean water.
  • 2. Apply soap and lather well.
  • 3. Scrub your hands for at least 20 seconds.
  • 4. Rinse your hands under clean water.
  • 5. Dry your hands with a clean towel or air dry them.
